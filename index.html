Lobster - Pebble - Seaweed

Rules

Lobster, Pebble, Seaweed is a game of chance. Select either lobster, pebble or seaweed to beat the computer's randomly selected choice.

Pebble beats Lobster, Seaweed beats Pebble, and Lobster beats Seaweed. If both you and the computer select the same item, the round is a tie, and play resumes until someone wins five rounds.
